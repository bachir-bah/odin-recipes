Cake recipe

Description of cake recipe

Hi, this is a recipe for a vanilla cake. It is a relatively easy recipe to follow, and will not take long to make. I hope you enjoy this website and recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 2 sticks unsalted butter, at room temperature, plus more for the pans
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for the pans
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk (or 3/4 cup heavy cream mixed with 1/2 cup water)
